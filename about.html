Who We Are

Marketing that actually works

B Social was founded by two sisters with backgrounds in marketing and the event space. We saw the same problem over and over: incredible brands doing amazing work, but completely invisible online.

We experienced it firsthand as brides ourselves — both planning weddings at the same time. We kept finding companies with beautiful work and great reputations that were nearly impossible to discover online. No Google presence. No social strategy. Totally invisible to the people who needed them most.

"We realized so many brands were losing customers simply because they weren't showing up when people searched or scrolled."

That experience lit the spark, but B Social isn't just for bridal. We work with brands across industries — med spas, wellness studios, beauty brands, e-commerce, and more. If you're a brand in a competitive market that deserves more visibility, we're your team.

Every strategy is hands-on, data-driven, and built specifically for your business. No templates, no runaround.
