From fragmented to fully scaled

How we rebuilt Filterbaby's ad account from scratch and scaled spend by 732% while increasing ROAS.

The Problem

A great product stuck in inefficient loops

When we took over Filterbaby's ad account, the structure was over-fragmented. Despite strong creative and real product demand, the account was stuck spinning its wheels.

  • Dozens of ad sets with overlapping audiences competing against each other
  • Learning phase constantly resetting — never getting enough data to optimize
  • Spend spread too thin across too many campaigns to generate meaningful signal
  • Inconsistent performance and slow optimization cycles

The bones were there. The product was great, the creative was solid. But the account architecture was holding everything back.

The Strategy

Rebuild, test, scale — in that order

01. Account Rebuild

Consolidated overlapping audiences, shifted to fewer high-signal ad sets, and centralized budget into scalable CBO frameworks. This let Meta's algorithm learn faster with real volume.

02. Creative Testing Engine

Launched 5–10 new ad concepts per week. Every brief was informed by thumbstop rate, CTR by angle, and add-to-cart-to-purchase drop-offs. Rapid iteration on hooks, problem framing, and lifestyle UGC.

03. Whitelisted Authority Content

Partnered with celebrity hair stylists, beauty creators, and dermatologists. Identified content that already performed organically and whitelisted it into the ad account as high-converting paid assets.

04. BFCM Promo Layer

During November, layered in BOGO offers, promo-first creative, urgency + scarcity framing, and retargeting support to push cold traffic into the funnel during peak demand.

Key Takeaways

What made this work

Structure matters more than creative. Filterbaby already had great creative. The problem was the account architecture — too many ad sets, not enough signal. Simplifying the structure unlocked everything.

Volume creates clarity. When you consolidate budget into fewer, smarter campaigns, the algorithm gets the data it needs to optimize. More signal, faster learning, better results.

Authority content scales trust. Whitelisting content from dermatologists and beauty creators gave us credibility we couldn't manufacture with brand-only creative. Trust at scale.
