The Problem

Great dentistry that patients never discover

Cosmetic dental practices do life-changing work. But if patients can't find you online, they'll book with whoever shows up first.

Competing with corporate chains

Large dental chains dominate search results with massive ad budgets and dozens of locations — pushing independent practices to the bottom of the page.

Patients compare before they call

Today's cosmetic dental patients read reviews, browse before-and-after photos, and compare multiple practices online before ever picking up the phone.

Stuck in the insurance model

Relying on insurance-based patients means lower margins and less cosmetic work. The high-value cash-pay patients are out there — but they're finding your competitors first.

Why This Matters

Cosmetic patients have the budget.
You need to be the one they find.

Cosmetic dental patients are high-intent searchers spending $5K–$50K+ on smile transformations — veneers, full-mouth restorations, Invisalign, implants. They're actively Googling, reading reviews, and choosing a provider right now. If your practice isn't showing up when they search "veneers near me" or "best cosmetic dentist," they'll book a consultation with your competitor instead. These aren't price shoppers. They're ready to invest.
